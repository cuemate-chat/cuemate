## Introduction

CueMate is an open-source intelligent interview assistant - an AI-driven real-time interview training tool that focuses on helping job seekers improve their interview skills and performance through systematic mock interview training and preparation.

### Installation Steps

1. Download the DMG installer for your chip type
2. Double-click the DMG file and follow the installation wizard (about 5 minutes)
3. Log in with the built-in account (username: `admin`, password: `cuemate`)
